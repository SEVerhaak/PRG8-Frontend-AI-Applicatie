How to Play

This game uses your webcam to detect hand gestures. When you click "Start Game", the system will begin tracking your hand movements.

"Simon says" will display a gesture. Try to copy it as accurately as possible within the time limit. If you get it right, you move to the next level!

Make sure your hand is well-lit and within the frame. Good luck!
